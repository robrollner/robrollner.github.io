The wonderful exciting afrobeat music to make your body shake and your mind groove

“Rhythm is what makes a good Afrobeat record,” says Gabriel Roth, DapTone Records Co-founder



From the clubs in Brooklyn, NY. The Afrobeat band Antibalas formed in 1998. A collection of musicians whose musical stylings vary wiht the sole purpose of bringing funky rythms and hypnotic grooves. Their lead vocalist and chief song writer Amayo draws inspiration from the late great Fela Kuti.

The band has released five albums, with their sixth album expected Summer 2017. They have toured extensively around the world playing festivals and clubs alike, even taking residence as the Carnegie Hall house band since 2014.
